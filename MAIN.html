Asus (ASUSTeK Computer Inc.), founded in 1989 in Taiwan, is a leading tech brand known for laptops, desktops, PC parts, and gaming devices. Its product lines include ZenBook (premium ultrabooks), VivoBook (budget-friendly), ROG and TUF (gaming), and ProArt (for creators). ASUS is praised for innovation, solid build quality, and wide price options, though after-sales service varies and gaming models often have average battery life. It’s especially popular among gamers and content creators.

Lenovo , founded in 1984 in China, is one of the world’s largest PC makers, known for its reliable and versatile laptops, desktops, and tablets. Its ThinkPad series is highly respected for durability and business use, while IdeaPad and Yoga target everyday users and 2-in-1 flexibility. The Legion series focuses on gaming, offering strong performance at competitive prices. Lenovo is praised for good value, strong build quality, and long battery life in many models, though some budget laptops may feel less premium. It remains a top choice for both professionals and casual users.

MSI (Micro-Star International) is a leading Taiwanese technology company renowned for its high-performance products tailored to gamers, creators, and professionals. Founded in 1986 and headquartered in New Taipei City, MSI has built a strong reputation in the global market with its cutting-edge laptops, motherboards, graphics cards, and gaming peripherals. The brand is especially popular in the eSports community, where its bold designs and powerful hardware—often marked by the iconic dragon logo—stand out for both style and speed. MSI continues to push boundaries in innovation, offering sleek, performance-driven solutions that cater to users who demand reliability and flair.

Acer Inc. is a Taiwanese multinational electronics company founded in 1976 and headquartered in New Taipei City. Known for its wide range of consumer and business tech products, Acer manufactures laptops, desktops, monitors, tablets, and accessories, with popular lines like Aspire, Swift, Nitro, and Predator catering to users from casual consumers to hardcore gamers. Over the years, Acer has evolved from a traditional hardware manufacturer into a forward-thinking brand focused on sustainability, innovation, and smart solutions. The company has also expanded into areas like cloud services, e-mobility, and AI-assisted technologies, while maintaining a strong presence in global markets through its commitment to affordability, reliability, and eco-conscious design.
