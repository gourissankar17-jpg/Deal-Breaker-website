About Deal Breaker


1. What is Deal Breaker?

Deal Breaker is a student-built price comparison platform that helps users find the best deals across online stores like Amazon and Flipkart.
Instead of juggling tabs and hunting for discounts, users get a clean, focused view of top products and prices—all in one place.


2. Why Did We Build It?

Born out of a semester project, Deal Breaker tackles a real-world problem: price hunting fatigue.
We wanted to create something practical, intuitive, and genuinely helpful—while sharpening our web development skills in the process.


3. How Does It Work?

Currently, Deal Breaker is a static site that showcases prices for selected products from Amazon and Flipkart.
Each product card includes platform-specific pricing and direct “Buy Now” links.
It simulates the experience of a real-time comparison engine, with plans to evolve.


4. Who Is It For?

Students, gamers, budget-conscious buyers—anyone who wants to shop smarter without wasting time.
Deal Breaker is built for everyday users who value clarity, speed, and savings.


5. What’s Next?

As of now, Deal Breaker is a learning project.
But we’re dreaming bigger: real-time price tracking, expanded product categories, and interactive features that make deal hunting fun and effortless.
